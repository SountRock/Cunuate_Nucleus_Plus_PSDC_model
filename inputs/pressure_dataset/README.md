## About the datasets

* `inputs\pressure_dataset\new_pressure_stim_2to1_RA_SA_ms1000` – dataset for **RA and SA distribution as in humans**.
* `inputs\pressure_dataset\new_pressure_stim_2to1_RA_SA_ms1000` – dataset for **RA and SA distribution as in mice**.
* `inputs\pressure_dataset\inputs_hard_pattern` – from the **model of ephaptic nerve coupling**.

The datasets can be divided into:

* **Regular** (mostly synchronous spikes)
* **Semi-regular** (50% synchronous, 50% asynchronous spikes)
* **Irregular** (mostly asynchronous patterns)

And also categorized by axon location:

* **Axon start**
* **Axon middle**
* **Axon end**
